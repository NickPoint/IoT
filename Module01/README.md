### What IoT entails

IoT is a wide topic that had many names before it became known as IoT, such as pervasive computing and automation. It has many real-world applications. 

It is a network of physical objects (devices) with sensors, software, and connectivity that collect and exchange data over the internet to automate tasks and improve decisions.


### IoT Domains

1. Smart Home - Automation of homes (lights, security, thermostats).
2. Industrial IoT (IIoT) - Machines and sensors in factories for monitoring and efficiency.
3. Healthcare IoT - Wearables and medical devices for patient tracking and remote care.


### Common Data Protocols

1. MQTT - Lightweight messaging protocol for small, low-power devices.
2. HTTP/REST - Standard web protocol used for sending data between devices and servers.

### Typical IoT Devices

1. Arduino / ESP32 - Microcontrollers used to build IoT projects.
2. Smart Thermostat - Appliance that controls temperature automatically via the internet.
